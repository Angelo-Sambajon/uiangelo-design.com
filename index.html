ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Angelo. Some background information about me is that I was born in the Philippines, but was mainly raised in Wellington, New Zealand.

I first found my passion for creativity through music. I played instruments such as the guitar, bass, piano and the drums being my main instrument. I feel in love with being able to create something that would please people.

However my love for being creative and design would grow bigger during my high school years. During the year 2018, we had decided to make the move to Auckland after living in Wellington for over 13 years. I had originally wanted to pursue architect, but unfortunately things didn’t work out. So I had a go with design and fell in love with create posters, mobile apps, banners, swing tags, and more. The thing that I loved doing out of all those things were when I was designing the website interface. Although at the time, they we’re that good, it sparked something in me that made me was to continue designing websites, and here we are I guess. Welcome to my portfolio.
