ABOUT ME

Hello, a brief introduction of myself to welcome you to my portfolio. My name is Angelo. Born in the Philippines and migrated with my parents and sibling to New Zealand. I consider myself a Wellingtonian, as we lived there for almost 13 years until we moved to Auckland.

My passion is playing musical instruments like guitar, bass, piano, and drums. Through music, I can express my creativity and feel accomplished when people appreciate my music. My passion for creativity and design has grown significantly during high school.

I learned to design business promotion materials, posters and banners, and it became my hobby. Because of my passion, I decided to pursue a career in interactive design, where I gained more knowledge and experience designing websites. My portfolio features some of my works showcasing my creativity. Enjoy.
